Bài kiểm tra dựa trên chuẩn TOEIC

Trải nghiệm cảm giác thi TOEIC với bài kiểm tra mới

 

PRACTICE FOR THE TOEIC*

 

Dựa trên cấu trúc đề thi TOEIC, bài kiểm tra này có tổng cộng 7 phần thi, bao gồm 4 phần Nghe và 3 phần Đọc. Đây là một bài mẫu giúp bạn chuẩn bị cho kì thi TOEIC, với số câu hỏi được cắt giảm còn một phần năm so với bài TOEIC thực tế.

 

Kết quả của bài kiểm tra này được đánh giá trên cơ sở phương pháp tính điểm của TOEIC. Điểm số bạn nhận được sẽ không đảm bảo điểm thi TOEIC thực tế của bạn, nhưng ít nhiều sẽ phản ánh khả năng đạt được một mức điểm tương đương.

 

BẠN ĐÃ SẴN SÀNG ĐẾN ĐÂU CHO BÀI THI TOEIC? KIỂM TRA NGAY!

 

Ghi chú: Để có kết quả chân thực, bạn nên cố gắng hoàn tất bài kiểm tra trong vòng 30 phút.

 

" *TOEIC © là sản phẩm được đăng ký bản quyền của Educational Testing Service (ETS). Đây chỉ là một bài kiểm tra dựa trên chuẩn TOEIC, chưa được sự xác nhận của ETS"

- Bạn cũng có thể nghe hiểu giọng phát âm tiếng Anh ở các vùng miền khác nhau.

- Bạn có thể xem TV cũng như nghe radio bằng tiếng Anh.

- Bạn có thể soạn thảo nhiều loại văn bản, kể cả văn bản có tính chất chuyên môn.

- Bạn không gặp bất cứ khó khăn nào khi nói và sử dụng tiếng Anh trong các tình huống giao tiếp ngoài xã hội cũng như mang tính chuyên môn.

- Bạn có thể soạn thảo những văn bản chuyên môn phức tạp, hiểu được ẩn ý và các nghĩa hàm ngôn.

- Bạn có thể sử dụng các cấu trúc tiếng Anh phức tạp và không gặp trở ngại gì với vốn từ vựng.

    PART I  PHOTOGRAPHS

    Directions: For each question, you will see a picture and you will hear four short statements. The statements will be spoken just one time. They cannot be played another time, so you must listen carefully to understand what the speaker says.

     

    When you hear the four statements, look at the picture and choose the statement that describes what you see in the picture. Then, click on the letter corresponding to the statement that most closely matches what you see. 
     

    a. They are taking a walk

    b. They are working on their laptops

    c. They are drinking coffee

    d. They are making pasta

     

     

     

    Now listen to the four statements. 

    Statement (b), "They are working on their laptops", best describes what you see in the picture. Therefore, you should choose answer (b).

     

     

    PART II QUESTIONS RESPONSES

     

    Directions: In this part of the test, you will hear a question spoken in English, followed by three responses, also spoken in English. The question and the response will be spoken just one time.

     

    They will not appear on your screen and they cannot be played another time so you must listen carefully to understand what the speakers say. You are to choose the best response to each question and click on the corresponding letter.

     

    Now listen to a sample question.

     

    When will you graduate?

    You will hear: 

    a. I’ll have my diploma this August.

    You will also hear:

    b. I’m applying for an internship next month.

    c. I’m still an undergraduate.

     

    The best response to the question “When will you graduate?” is choice (a) I’ll have my diploma this August.

    Therefore, you should choose answer (a)

     

    PART III SHORT CONVERSATIONS

     

    Directions: In this part of the test, you will hear 6 conversations between two people. The conversations will not appear on your screen and you will hear the conversations once and they cannot be played another time, so you must listen carefully to understand what the speakers say.

     

    You will read a question about each conversation. The question will be followed by four answers. You are to choose the best answer to each question and click on the corresponding letter.

     

    PART IV SHORT TALKS

     

    Directions: In this part of the test, you will hear one short passage. It will not appear on your screen and it will be spoken just one time. It cannot be played another time, so you must listen carefully to understand and remember what is said.

     

    Then, you will read four questions about the short passage. The questions will be followed by four answers. You are to choose the best answer to each question and click on the corresponding letter.

     

    PART V INCOMPLETE SENTENCES

     

    Directions: Questions 21-28 are incomplete sentences. Four words or phrases are given beneath each sentence. You are to choose the one word or phrase that best completes the sentence, and click on it.

     

    You will read:

    This player has done a terrific championship, _________, he will drafted for the national team.

     

    a. hoping.

    b. hopeful

    c. hopefully

    d. hope

     

    This sentence should read, “This player has done a terrific championship, hopefully, he will be drafted for the national team”.

     

    Therefore, you should answer (c).

     

    Now begin work on the questions.

     

    PART VI ERROR RECOGNITION

     

    Directions: In questions 29-32, each sentence has four words or phrases underlined. You are to identify one underlined word or phrase that should be corrected or rewritten, and click on it.

     

    Example:

    Everybody know (a) that Bengali tigers are an (b) endangered species (c), and they are becoming extinct (d).

     

    The underline word “know” is not correct in this sentence. This sentence should read, “Everybody knows that Bengali tigers are an endangered species, and they are becoming extinct”. Therefore, you should chose answer (a).

     

    Now begin work on the questions. 

     

    PART VII READING COMPREHENSION

     

    Directions: Questions 33-40 are based on selection of reading materials, such as notices, letters, forms, newspaper and magazine articles and advertisements. You are to choose the one best answer to each question, and then click on it. Answer all question following each reading selection on the basis of what is stated or implied in that selection.

     

    Read the following example.

     

    Air New Zealand has ordered 14 aircraft from European plane maker Airbus.

    The order includes 10 A320neo, one A320 and three A321 aircraft, the airline's chief Christopher Luxon said at an industry conference in Doha.

    The planes have a combined list price of about $1.5bn (£900m; 1.1bn euros), although when airlines bulk order planes, they always secure a significant discount.

    The majority of the planes are due to be delivered between 2017 and 2019.

     

    Question: Which of the following is NOT true?

     

    a. Air New Zealand has ordered ten A320neo, one A320 and three A321 from Airbus.

    b. The total price is £1.5 billion.

    c. Many of the planes are due to be delivered from 2017 to 2019.

    d. Christopher Luxon is the chief of Air New Zealand.

     

    The reading selection says that the total price is £1.5 billion. Therefore, you should choose answer (b).

     

    Now begin work on the questions.
